WHO AM I

A short, human-readable page — not a résumé. The goal is clarity: what I’m building, why it matters, and how to reach me.

Builder
Systems thinker
Language + Meaning
Truth-seeking
Energy + Practical tech

In one paragraph

I build tools that turn confusion into structure. That includes language systems (meaning, translation, scripture analysis), and practical engineering experiments (energy, efficiency, prototypes). I care about integrity: show the evidence, reveal the pattern, improve the method.

What I’m doing right now

  • Building “y”: a semantic mirror for studying texts using local lexical resources.
  • Exploring how cultures encode meaning through structure, density, and organization of writing.
  • Prototyping clean workflows: repeatable scripts, readable corpora, measurable results.

What I believe

  • Truth improves when it can be tested.
  • Words should speak for themselves — we don’t need to “judge” them to learn.
  • Interpretation is powerful; it should be transparent and accountable.

What I can offer

  • Rapid prototyping (scripts → datasets → search → UI)
  • Semantic/lexical reasoning (WordNet-style structures, glosses, relations)
  • Clear communication: turning messy ideas into usable artifacts

What I’m looking for

  • Collaborators who care about truth, language, and systems.
  • Support for building public-facing tools (UI, hosting, design, governance).
  • People who can challenge the work without ego.
