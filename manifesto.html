Project 67

Manifesto

Project 67 is a signal of love in a noisy world. It exists to reduce loneliness, increase understanding, and train the future toward coherence. We become what we communicate.

Love is advanced Verification is care Be the signal Return to coherence
1) Be the signal
Don’t amplify fear for entertainment. Don’t feed vanity for approval. Create what makes the world more human.
2) Love is advanced intelligence
Fear is simple. Love takes patience, modelling, and courage. Choose the higher-information path even when it’s harder.
3) Verification is care
Reality-checking isn’t cold; it’s compassionate. Truth reduces harm. Honest feedback is protection.
4) Reduce loneliness
The first enemy is isolation. Build work that reaches people where they are. Make connection normal again.
5) No cruelty as entertainment
Darkness can exist in the art — but never as celebration. We don’t trade humanity for clicks.
6) Build coherence
Every release should move from chaos toward meaning. More clarity. More integration. Less noise.
7) The self is a template
“Your Face Here” reminds us: identity isn’t vanity. The point is what we carry, what we teach, what we pass on.
8) Repeat the symbols
The signal strengthens through repetition. Let “67” return again and again — until it becomes familiar.
9) Make it usable
Don’t build fragile pipelines that collapse. Build systems that survive low budgets, low energy, and real life.
10) Leave the world better
The test is simple: did we increase dignity, understanding, and connection? If yes — we’re aligned.
