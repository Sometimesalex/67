WHO AM I

This page describes the human side of Project 67. The companion page — Who are you? — describes the system.

In one paragraph

I build tools that turn confusion into structure. That includes language systems (meaning, translation, scripture analysis) and practical engineering experiments (energy, efficiency, prototypes). I care about integrity: show evidence, reveal patterns, improve methods.

What I’m doing

  • Building “y”: a semantic mirror for studying text.
  • Exploring how cultures encode meaning.
  • Creating repeatable, transparent workflows.

What I believe

  • Truth improves when tested.
  • Words should speak for themselves.
  • Interpretation must be accountable.

What I offer

  • Rapid prototyping
  • Semantic reasoning
  • Turning ideas into artifacts

What I’m seeking

  • Serious collaborators
  • People who challenge ideas cleanly
  • Support for public-facing tools

Human ↔ System

This project is built on a feedback loop: human asks, system reflects. Neither replaces the other. One provides intent. The other provides structure. Together they form a mirror.
